The aim of, New world academy is to prepare boys & girls students well equipped with scientific knowledge, who will face the challenges of the modern world in a befitting manner. It lays special emphasis on character building education convent style and infuses in its student all those qualities which lead them to become useful citizen of this Islamic republic.

Our challenging curriculum prepares students for an internationally recognized examination offered by Cambridge University which enables them to gain admission in the finest universities both in Pakistan and abroad.

New world academy for Boys & Girls is a private institution and runs. On non-commercial and non-political basis. It has well equipped science labs and above all has The services of highly qualified and devoted lectures. It is housed in a spacious building situated in the heart of the shah faisal town & abounded by big shopping centers which is in the central most & easily accessible place.

The school achieves these goals by offering the following:

  • An environment of caring and respect where every child is encouraged to gain confidence and self-knowledge and to achieve his/her full potential.
  • An Inquiry based approach in the primary classes with an emphasis on critical thinking skills, and a balanced program which ensures that students are well grounded in the basic skills of reading, writing and mathematics.
  • A thorough preparation in all subject areas is provided in the Senior school with an emphasis on conceptual understanding, which enables students to excel in institutions of higher education.
